A Little Bit about Its History

The first international football match was held in Glasgow,
Scotland in 1872, in which the Scottish team and the English team competed.
In 1904, FIFA was established, and shortly after its establishment, it tried to organize an international
soccer tournament between the various countries outside the Olympic framework, which was to be held in Switzerland in 1906.
